# Spooky night

mi22041 – Ognjen Marković

A 3D night scene featuring a military truck parked in front of a house, with functional headlights illuminating the environment. Two seconds after the headlights are turned off, a rocking chair appears and starts gently swinging. The project demonstrates lighting models, animation, and interactive real-time scene control.
## Controls

W / A / S / D → move the camera forward, left, backward, right

Mouse → rotate the camera (increased sensitivity)

T → toggle truck headlights on/off

ESC → exit the application
